Artificial Intelligence : Test Your Knowledge On This Fun Website 🤖


Overview

Welcome to the AI website where you can test your knowledge based on how much you know about Artificial Intelligence by navigating through this website. This site contains a brief summary of what AI is about as well as a quiz-based game you can play for fun.

How It Works

To obtain resources on the subject that will help you get ready for the game, select the "Notes" tab from the menu bar.

After that, you can test your understanding of the information by playing the game.

To access the game, simply follow the steps below:

1.Navigation

Navigate to the menu bar and click on "Game".

2.Start

Click on "Start Game" button to begin.

3.Game Instructions

The game consists of 25 multiple choice questions and each question is worth four points.Choose one option you feel is the answer. Please note that wrong answers will not be counted towards your final score so choose wisely. Again, this is just for fun; so it shouldn't matter whether you win or lose.

GO AHEAD! HAVE FUN!!
