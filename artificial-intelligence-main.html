Artificial Intelligence : Test Your Knowledge On This Fun Website 🤖


Overview

Welcome to my very own interactive game site. Here, you can test your knowledge on how well you know about Artificial Intelligence by navigating through this website. This site contains a brief summary of what AI is about as well as a quiz-based game you can play for fun.

How It Works

A brief outline is located at the "Notes" tab towards the top of this website

To play the game, simply follow the steps below:

1.Navigation

Go to the navigation bar located towards the top of this website and click on "Game".

2.Start

Click on "Start Game" button to begin.

3.Game Instructions

The game consists of 25 multiple choice questions and each question is worth four points.Choose one option you feel is the answer. Please note that wrong answers will not be counted towards your final score so choose wisely. Again, this is just for fun; so it shouldn't matter whether you win or lose.

GO AHEAD! HAVE FUN!!
